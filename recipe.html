The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and tastes great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 sliced bread
  • 1 jar or grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • butter knife
  • sharp knife
  • cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread together on the cutting board
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. On the other slice of bread, spead a layer of jelly
  4. Put the two slices together
  5. Cut the slices in half with the sharp knife
  6. Enjoy!!
